Need help with the app? We're happy to assist.

## Contact

Email us at [info@buffalointeractive.ai](mailto:info@buffalointeractive.ai) and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I change what I'm tracking?**
Go to Settings (gear icon on the home screen) and tap your tracked drink to edit it.

**Does my data sync across devices?**
No — Tap & Track is local-only. Your data stays on your device and is not backed up to the cloud.

**How do I delete my data?**
Deleting the app removes all data permanently. There is no account to delete.

**I lost my data after reinstalling. Can I recover it?**
Unfortunately, no. Because all data is stored locally on the device, it cannot be recovered after the app is deleted.

**I purchased the app but it's showing as locked after reinstalling.**
Tap "Restore Purchase" on the paywall screen to recover your purchase.

**The widget isn't updating.**
Try removing the widget from your home screen and re-adding it. If the issue persists, contact us.

**How do I delete a logged entry?**
Tap the bar for any day on the home screen chart to open the day's log. Swipe left on any entry to delete it.
